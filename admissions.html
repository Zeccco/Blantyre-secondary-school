What You Need

Admission Requirements

Ensure you have all the necessary documents and qualifications before applying

Academic Requirements

  • Completed Standard 8 (for Form 1)
  • Minimum average of 65% in core subjects
  • Passing grades in English and Mathematics
  • Good conduct report from previous school
  • For Form 3: Completed Form 2 with good grades

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copy of birth certificate
  • Last 2 years of school reports
  • 2 passport-sized photos
  • Recommendation letter from headteacher
  • Immunization record

Fees & Payments

  • Application fee: MWK 5,000 (non-refundable)
  • Registration fee: MWK 25,000 (one-time)
  • Tuition: MWK 150,000 per term
  • Activity fee: MWK 15,000 per term
  • Boarding: MWK 200,000 per term (optional)
How to Apply

Admission Process in 5 Simple Steps

Follow these steps to complete your application successfully

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form or download and submit a physical copy to our admissions office. Include all required documents and pay the application fee.

Takes: 20-30 minutes Form available below
2

Entrance Examination

All applicants must take our entrance exam in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge. Exams are held on scheduled dates throughout the year.

Next exam: April 15, 2026 Duration: 3 hours
3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents/guardians are invited for an interview with our admissions panel to discuss the student's goals and fit for the school.

Panel: Headteacher & 2 teachers Duration: 30 minutes
4

Admission Decision

Successful candidates receive an offer letter within 2 weeks after the interview. The letter includes details about placement, fees, and next steps.

Sent via email and post Response time: 2 weeks
5

Enrollment & Registration

Accept the offer by paying the registration fee and submitting any remaining documents. Attend orientation day and begin your journey at BSS.

Orientation: January 10, 2027 Registration due within 30 days

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the academic year start?

The academic year begins in January (Term 1), followed by Term 2 in May, and Term 3 in September. New Form 1 students start with an orientation week in early January.

Is boarding accommodation available?

Yes, we offer boarding facilities for students who live far from the school. Separate hostels for boys and girls with house parents, study areas, and meals provided.

Can I apply if I'm not from Blantyre?

Absolutely! We welcome students from all districts of Malawi and even international students. Boarding is available for those who need accommodation.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer a limited number of scholarships and bursaries based on academic merit and financial need. Apply early and indicate your interest in financial aid.

What if I miss the entrance exam date?

Contact our admissions office immediately. We may be able to schedule a make-up exam or invite you to the next available exam date, depending on space availability.

Do I need to take an exam for Form 3 admission?

Yes, all applicants must take entrance exams, including those applying for Form 3. The exam covers the core subjects appropriate for the level.
