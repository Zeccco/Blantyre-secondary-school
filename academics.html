Our curriculum balances traditional academic subjects with modern skills development, ensuring students are well-equipped for the challenges of the 21st century. Our Programs

Academic Programs

Structured learning pathways designed for student success

Junior Secondary

Form 1 & 2

Duration: 2 Years

Core Subjects: English, Mathematics, Chichewa, Science, Social Studies, Life Skills

Electives: Introductory choices from all departments

Focus: Building foundational knowledge, study skills, and subject exploration

Average class size: 35 35 periods per week
Senior Secondary

Form 3 & 4

Duration: 2 Years

Core Subjects: English, Mathematics (all students)

Specialization: Students choose 6-8 subjects based on career interests

Combinations: Sciences, Humanities, Commerce, or Mixed

Preparation: MSCE (Malawi School Certificate of Education)

Average class size: 30 95% pass rate
Post-Secondary

Form 5 (Coming Soon)

Duration: 1-2 Years

Programs planned: A-Level equivalent, Pre-University Foundation

Subjects: Advanced Sciences, Mathematics, Humanities

Target: University preparation and international examinations

University pathway Launching 2027
How We Teach

Curriculum & Teaching Approach

Our balanced curriculum combines academic rigor with holistic development

Knowledge-Based Learning

Strong foundation in core subjects with emphasis on understanding concepts, not just memorization.

Practical Application

Hands-on experiments, projects, and real-world applications of classroom learning.

Collaborative Learning

Group projects, discussions, and peer learning to develop teamwork and communication.

Technology Integration

Digital tools, e-learning platforms, and computer-based research in all subjects.

Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, assignments, and feedback to track progress and identify areas for improvement.

Critical Thinking

Problem-solving activities and analytical tasks that develop higher-order thinking skills.

Assessment

Examinations & Assessment

Preparing students for national exams and beyond

Internal Assessments

  • Weekly Tests: Every Friday
  • Monthly Tests: End of each month
  • Mid-Term Exams: After 8 weeks
  • End of Term Exams: November, April, August

National Examinations

  • JCE: Junior Certificate Exam (Form 2)
  • MSCE: Malawi School Certificate (Form 4)
  • Exam Board: MANEB
  • Exam Period: October - November

Performance Tracking

  • Individual progress reports
  • Parent-teacher conferences
  • Remedial classes for improvement
  • Honor roll recognition
