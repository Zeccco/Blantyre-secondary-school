Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Can I visit the school without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the right staff member is available to assist you. Contact our front office to arrange a visit.

Do you have weekend office hours?

Yes, our office is open on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM for admissions inquiries and general information. We are closed on Sundays.

How can I get application forms?

Application forms can be downloaded from our Admissions page, picked up in person at our office, or requested via email. We'll send them to you electronically.

Is there parking available for visitors?

Yes, we have visitor parking available at the front of the school. Security will direct you to available spots upon arrival.

Can I speak with a teacher directly?

For parent-teacher communication, please contact the Academic Office to schedule a meeting. Teachers are available during designated consultation hours.
